Privacy Policy

隐私政策

Your photos and processing data stay on your device. Celvion Stack requires no account, contains no ads or tracking, and does not upload your photos to developer servers.

你的照片与处理数据留在设备上。Celvion Stack 不要求账户、不投放广告、不进行跟踪，也不会把照片上传到开发者服务器。

Effective July 14, 2026生效日期：2026 年 7 月 14 日Version 1.0

Core commitment. Photo decoding, alignment, stacking, editing, and Core ML inference happen on your device. Celvion Stack does not operate a photo-processing server.

1. Scope

This policy applies to the Celvion Stack app for iOS and iPadOS and to this official website. Celvion Stack is an on-device tool for photo stacking, RAW image processing, color adjustment, denoising, and super-resolution enhancement.

2. Information the App Accesses and Why

Celvion Stack accesses information only as needed to provide features you choose to use.

  • Photos and files you select: image pixels, file names, dimensions, color information, and capture metadata included with the file may be used to import, align, stack, edit, enhance, preview, and export your images. The app does not browse other content without your selection or permission.
  • Photo Library add permission: used only when you choose to save a processed result to Apple Photos.
  • App preferences and recovery state: language, export options, performance preferences, and a brief interrupted-task state are stored on your device to remember your choices and improve reliability.
  • Device operating conditions: available memory, processor capability, Low Power Mode, thermal state, and available disk space are used only on device to tune performance, reduce heat or memory risk, and warn before large exports.
  • Local operational logs: the app may keep startup times and processing-stage information that does not contain photo content inside its sandbox to help diagnose startup or interrupted-processing issues. This information is not sent to the developer automatically.

3. On-Device Processing, iCloud, and File Providers

Photo decoding, alignment, stacking, editing, and Core ML model inference take place on your device. Celvion Stack does not upload photos, RAW files, or processed results to servers operated by the developer.

If an item you select is not stored locally, Apple Photos, iCloud Drive, or a file provider you choose may download it at your request. That transfer is handled by the relevant system or provider under its own privacy policy; it is not a transfer from Celvion Stack to the developer.

4. Collection, Tracking, and Sharing

Celvion Stack currently requires no account and includes no advertising, cross-app tracking, third-party analytics SDK, or developer-operated remote diagnostics service. The developer does not collect, sell, or share your photos, profile information, device identifiers, or app usage history.

When you use the system share sheet or export features to send a result to another app, cloud drive, website, or person, you initiate that transfer. The destination handles the content under its own terms and privacy practices.

5. Storage and Retention

  • Original photos and files remain at the source you selected. Celvion Stack does not modify the originals.
  • Temporary files and local caches may be created during processing. They are removed when a task finishes, during cleanup on a later launch, or when the operating system reclaims storage.
  • Language, export preferences, performance settings, local recovery state, and startup logs may remain in the app sandbox until updated, cleared after recovery, or removed when you uninstall the app.
  • Exported results remain in the Photos library or Files location you choose until you delete them.
  • Where supported by the output format, an export may retain technical metadata from the source image, potentially including capture time, camera information, or location already present in the original file. Review or remove metadata before sharing when appropriate.

6. This Website

This website does not offer accounts, run advertising, use analytics, or set tracking cookies. The language selector uses only the page URL parameter and does not create a user profile.

The site is hosted by GitHub Pages. Like most hosting providers, GitHub may process connection information such as IP addresses and request logs to deliver and secure the service. That processing is controlled by GitHub under its privacy statement.

If you email support, your email provider and the receiving email service process the message you choose to send. The developer uses it to respond to your request and retains it only as reasonably needed for support, legal, or security purposes.

7. Your Choices and Controls

  • You choose which photos or files to process and can change Photos access in iOS Settings.
  • You can cancel an import or processing task and delete exported results yourself.
  • Because the developer operates no app account system or remote app database, there is no server-side app profile for the developer to retrieve or delete. Uninstalling the app removes its local settings, caches, recovery state, and logs. Results saved to Photos or Files must be deleted separately by you.
  • You may contact the developer about a support email you previously sent and request deletion when applicable.

8. Children’s Privacy

Celvion Stack is not designed to collect personal information from children and does not ask any user to create an account or submit profile information. Children should use photo processing and sharing features with guidance from a parent or guardian.

9. Security

The app relies on the iOS sandbox, system permissions, and device security features to isolate local data. No storage or software system can guarantee absolute security, so protect your device, keep backups of important originals, and review content and metadata before sharing.

10. Changes to This Policy

If the app’s features, the website, or data practices change, this policy and its effective date will be updated. If a change materially affects your rights in the app, an appropriate notice will be provided in the app.

核心承诺。照片解码、对齐、堆栈、编辑以及 Core ML 推理均在你的设备上完成。Celvion Stack 不运营照片处理服务器。

一、适用范围

本政策适用于 Celvion Stack iOS 与 iPadOS App，以及本官方网站。Celvion Stack 是一款用于照片堆栈、RAW 图像处理、颜色调整、降噪和超分辨率增强的本地工具。

二、App 访问的信息及用途

Celvion Stack 只在提供你主动使用的功能所需范围内访问信息。

  • 你选择的照片和文件：包括图像像素、文件名、尺寸、色彩信息及文件附带的拍摄元数据，用于导入、对齐、堆栈、编辑、AI 增强、预览和导出。App 不会在未经你选择或授权的情况下浏览其他内容。
  • 照片图库写入权限：仅在你选择“保存到照片”时，用于把处理结果添加到系统照片图库。
  • App 设置与恢复状态：语言、导出选项、性能偏好，以及处理中断后的简短恢复状态保存在设备上，用于记住选择并改善稳定性。
  • 设备运行状态：可用内存、处理器能力、低电量模式、温度状态和可用磁盘空间仅在设备上用于调整性能、降低过热或内存不足风险，并在大型导出前提供提示。
  • 本地运行日志：App 可能在自身沙盒内保存不含照片内容的启动时间与运行阶段信息，以协助判断启动或处理中断问题；这些信息不会自动发送给开发者。

三、本地处理、iCloud 与文件提供方

照片解码、对齐、堆栈、编辑以及 Core ML AI 模型推理均在你的设备上完成。Celvion Stack 不会把照片、RAW 文件或处理结果上传到开发者运营的服务器。

如果你选择的照片或文件尚未保存在设备本地，iOS“照片”、iCloud Drive 或你选择的文件提供方可能根据你的操作下载该项目。该传输由相应系统或文件提供方处理，并受其隐私政策约束；它不属于 Celvion Stack 向开发者传输数据。

四、收集、跟踪与共享

Celvion Stack 当前不要求注册账户，不包含广告、跨 App 跟踪、第三方分析 SDK 或开发者运营的远程诊断服务。开发者不收集、出售或共享你的照片、个人资料、设备标识符或 App 使用记录。

当你通过系统分享菜单或导出功能把结果发送到其他 App、云盘、网站或联系人时，该操作由你主动发起，接收方将按照其自身规则处理内容。

五、存储与保留

  • 原始照片和文件保留在你选择的来源位置，Celvion Stack 不会修改原件。
  • 处理中可能创建临时文件和本地缓存。它们会在任务结束、下一次启动清理流程或系统回收存储空间时删除。
  • 语言、导出偏好、性能设置、本地恢复状态和启动日志可能保留在 App 沙盒内，直至被更新、完成恢复清理或你卸载 App。
  • 导出的结果保留在你选择的“照片”或“文件”位置，直至你自行删除。
  • 在格式支持的情况下，导出文件可能保留原图技术元数据，其中可能包括原文件已有的拍摄时间、相机信息或位置信息。分享前请根据需要检查或移除相关元数据。

六、本网站

本网站不提供账户、不投放广告、不使用分析服务，也不设置跟踪 Cookie。语言切换仅使用页面网址参数，不会建立用户画像。

本网站由 GitHub Pages 托管。和大多数托管服务一样，GitHub 可能为提供与保护服务而处理 IP 地址、请求日志等连接信息。相关处理由 GitHub 依据其隐私声明负责。

如果你发送支持邮件，你使用的邮件服务和收件服务会处理你主动提供的内容。开发者仅为回复请求而使用相关信息，并只在支持、法律或安全目的合理需要的期限内保留。

七、你的选择与控制

  • 你可以只选择需要处理的照片或文件，并可在 iOS“设置”中修改照片权限。
  • 你可以取消导入或处理任务，并自行删除导出结果。
  • 由于开发者没有 App 账户系统或远程 App 数据库，因此没有可供开发者查询或删除的服务器端 App 个人资料。卸载 App 会移除其本地设置、缓存、恢复状态和日志；保存在照片图库或文件夹中的导出结果需要你单独删除。
  • 对于你此前主动发送的支持邮件，可在适用情况下联系开发者请求删除。

Celvion Stack 不以收集儿童个人信息为目的，也不会要求任何用户创建账户或提交个人资料。儿童应在父母或监护人指导下使用照片处理和分享功能。

九、信息安全

App 使用 iOS 沙盒、系统权限和设备安全机制隔离本地数据。任何存储或软件系统都无法保证绝对安全，因此请妥善保护设备、备份重要原片，并在分享文件前检查内容与元数据。

十、政策更新
