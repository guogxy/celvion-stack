Purpose: optional on-device image denoising using NAFNet-SIDD width64 and width32 models. The app prefers width64 for quality and can fall back to width32 or Core Image processing.

Source project: megvii-research/NAFNet

RealESRGAN-x4plus Core ML ModelBSD-3-Clause

Purpose: optional 4× on-device image super-resolution using the RRDBNet RealESRGAN_x4plus architecture.

Original project: xinntao/Real-ESRGAN
Core ML source: RealESRGAN-CoreML
Copyright: © 2021 Xintao Wang
License: BSD 3-Clause.

The model accepts a 1 × 3 × 256 × 256 Float16 RGB tensor and produces a 1 × 3 × 1024 × 1024 result. The AI path requires iOS 18 or later and falls back to local non-AI processing when unavailable.

用途：使用 RRDBNet RealESRGAN_x4plus 架构，在设备端进行可选的 4× 图像超分辨率处理。

原始项目：xinntao/Real-ESRGAN
Core ML 来源：RealESRGAN-CoreML
版权：© 2021 Xintao Wang
许可证：BSD 3-Clause。

模型接收 1 × 3 × 256 × 256 Float16 RGB 张量，并生成 1 × 3 × 1024 × 1024 结果。AI 路径需要 iOS 18 或更高版本；不可用时会回退至本地非 AI 处理。
